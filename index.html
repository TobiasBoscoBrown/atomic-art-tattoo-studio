Questions

Good to know

Do I need an appointment or can I walk in?+

Atomic Art is appointment only. The best first step is a free consultation. Call Brian at (208) 466-8710 or text Brenda LuWho at (208) 250-0564 to set one up.

Do you do custom designs or only flash?+

Everything is custom. Brian draws your piece from scratch based on your idea and references, and you approve the art before any ink goes in.

Can you cover up or fix an old tattoo?+

Yes. Cover ups and reworks are a specialty. You get an honest assessment of what is possible at your free consult, then a design built to hide the old ink.

How much does a tattoo cost?+

Pricing depends on size, detail and placement. Regulars describe the pricing as fair and the quality as the best in the Treasure Valley. You get a clear quote at your consultation.
